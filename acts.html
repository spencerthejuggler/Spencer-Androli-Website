Acts

Flynn Creek Circus 2023

I performed these acts in the Flynn Creek Circus 2023 summer and winter seasons. I performed the balls and ring act in the first half of the show and then the club act in the second half of the show. My character in the show was a MIB government agent that made the crowd forget about the aliens that showed up previously in the show with my neurolizer. It was truly such a fun character to play and always received an overwhelming amount of support from the audience. Throughout the pretty much 6 months of playing this character, I definitely learned so much about performing in all conditions (extreme heat, cold, rain, altitude, and exhaustion - just because we are in a tent doesn't mean those factors don't affect the act). I also throughout the tour I added some flair to the acts that really added to them. It is a slightly different act now than what it started off as. I am of course still performing this act and I have it in my pocket to perform it anywhere, although I am striving to create other acts with different feels to them rather than just a hype show-offy act.

Balls Assemble Act (Rooted by Circus Evo Show)

This is an ensemble ball juggling act. I do most of the juggling but I worked alongside some other artists to create it. It was so much fun performing this act in both Hamlet and Greensboro, NC. It was created in along with a 75 minute show in a 3 week creation process. I think we did pretty good.
