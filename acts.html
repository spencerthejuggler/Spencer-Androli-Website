Acts

Flynn Creek Circus 2023

I performed these acts in the Flynn Creek Circus 2023 summer and winter seasons. I performed the balls and ring act in the first half of the show and then the club act in the second half of the show. My character in the show was a MIB government agent that made the crowd forget about the aliens that showed up previously in the show with my neurolizer. It was truly such a fun character to play and always received an overwhelming amount of support from the audience. Throughout the pretty much 6 months of playing this character, I definitely learned so much about performing in all conditions (extreme heat, cold, rain, altitude, and exhaustion - just because we are in a tent doesn't mean those factors don't affect the act). I also throughout the tour I added some flair to the acts that really added to them. It is a slightly different act now than what it started off as. I am of course still performing this act and I have in my pocket to perform it anywhere although I am striving to create other acts with different feels to them rather than just a hype show-offy act.

Balls Assemble Act (Rooted by Circus Evo Show)

This is an ensemble ball juggling act. I do most of the juggling but I worked alongside some other artists to create it. It was so much fun performing this act in both Hamlet and Greensboro, NC. It was created in along with a 75 minute show in a 3 week creation process. I think we did pretty good. Hope you all enjoy this act!

Hideaway Circus Solo Ball Juggling Act

This was an open air outdoor circus with whom I toured 5 months with. I definitely had a blast performing this act as the kind of cool high school jock character that the clown of the show looked up to. This act is definitely an act meant to highlight confidence. I had a blast performing this! I will definitely miss this act.

Ps. Please ignore the plane engine sounds and the chatter. We were performing beside an airport so it was kind of loud in that sense. But I rather keep the audience reactions in rather than cover them up with the audio track of music.

Hideaway Circus Colorful Club Juggling Act

Here is a second juggling act I would do as part of the Hideaway Circus Canvas Sky show with 4 other people. This was truly an act I always looked forward to in the show because I got to do it with other people. It was quite enjoyable and fun.

This act kind of acts as part of the resolution of the show where the clown starts to realize that he can shine and be cool by just being who he is with his own skills.

This act also has a subtle message of society and how when negativity creeps in on a group it can spread and that we must at times insert some positivity back in to bring back joy and a "colorful" and vibrant environment.
